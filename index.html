Clarification

Claude is in Copilot now

What that does — and does not — mean.

What it does

A powerful everyday engine

Copilot now runs on a Claude model, reasoning over whatever it can reach. Great for broad, everyday work.

What it doesn't

Not a governed source

It can reach ungoverned content, so its answers aren't validated or attributed for use in front of a customer.

Claude in Copilot is its own thing — a better engine, not a governed source.

© 2026 Equinix, Inc.
Access

Getting access

Who has it, who doesn't, and how to get it.

Who has it

Most commercial M365 tenants, on by default — Claude Opus 4.8 in the model selector across Copilot Chat, Excel, PowerPoint, and Copilot Studio.

Who doesn't

EU/EFTA, UK, and government or sovereign cloud tenants — Anthropic models are off by default there.

What you need

A Copilot license plus admin enablement: M365 admin center → Copilot → Settings → AI providers → Anthropic.

Two tells: Copilot shows a UI indicator when a Claude model is in use. And some preview models carry Anthropic data retention — check before pasting sensitive data.

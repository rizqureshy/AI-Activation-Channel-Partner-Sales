# CRO AI Activation Community — Website Change Log

**Branch:** `site/cro-ai-activation-community`
**Period:** from the navigation simplification onward (June 2026)

A summary of changes made to the community website since we streamlined the
site down to a focused set of pages. Grouped by area, newest work included.

---

## Navigation & site structure
- **Simplified the navigation to 9 focused pages** (removed the cluttered menu
  and the separate quick-links bar): Home · Join the Community · Community
  Champions · AI Events Calendar · AI Clinic · Community Gallery · Skill Up,
  Speed Up · Learning Lanes · AI Activation for Teams.
- Single-line top navigation; the **"Join the Community"** item is now a
  highlighted colored button, consistent with the call-to-action style used
  across the site.

## Entry experience
- Added a **welcome "door" splash screen** — visitors land on a frosted-glass
  door with a single **"Open Community"** button; opening it swings the doors
  back and reveals the site. This also doubles as the moment we start the
  background music (browsers only allow sound after a click).
- Refined the door to **open slowly and realistically**, like a real set of
  doors swinging on their hinges.
- Added a **background soundtrack** at 50% volume, with a mute/unmute control.

## Home page
- **Recomposed the hero** along modern landing-page best practices: one clear
  headline, concise copy, two primary actions, and a warm, human tone.
- Added a **rotating "Your … matters" line** (Your Story · Your Questions ·
  Your Experiments · Your Work · Your Fun · You matter) that animates in place.
- Renamed the secondary action to **"Community Projects Gallery."**
- Switched the "Join" action from Microsoft Teams to the **Viva Engage**
  community.

## Join the Community
- Reworked into a **short, warm, welcoming page** ("You belong here") with the
  same animated hero treatment and a clear Viva Engage join action.
- Particle artwork now forms a **heart**, matching the "you belong" message.

## Community Champions
- Removed placeholder/sample content; kept a clean
  **"Hall of Fame of our Community Champions — refreshed every month"** line.

## AI Clinic
- **Refocused the page on its real purpose: booking a 1:1 consultation** with
  a Community Expert to talk through use-cases, ideas, and challenges.
- Added a **booking tool**: pick an available **Wednesday slot (12:30–1:00 PM
  ET)**, enter name, email, and a short use-case note. On booking it generates
  a **calendar invite** for both the requester and the clinic host, and opens a
  pre-filled notification email.
- **Tightened the page** — removed extra cards; the page now goes hero →
  booking, with the same rotating-line animation as the home page.
- Particle artwork forms a **medical "+" sign** that gently drifts/hovers.

## Skill Up, Speed Up (learning videos)
- Rebuilt as a **curated AI learning library** organized into four tracks —
  **Prompt Engineering, Beyond the Basics, LLMs & RAG, and AI Agents** — each
  tagged Basic / Intermediate / Advanced, using well-regarded videos
  (Karpathy, 3Blue1Brown, IBM, freeCodeCamp, Jeff Su) plus our community films.
- Added **in-page video playback** — clicking a video plays it right on the
  page in an overlay; it never sends people off-site. (Music auto-pauses while
  a video plays.)
- Particle artwork forms a **hovering, slowly rotating play icon.**

## Learning Lanes
- Tightened into the **GTM Ops "AI Upskilling Pathway"**: a clear two-tier path
  (proficiency floor + recognized Microsoft certifications) presented as tidy,
  easy-to-scan course cards with provider, level, cost, outcome, and links.
- Particle artwork forms an **ascending staircase** that warms in color as it
  climbs — representing the upskilling journey.

## AI Activation for Teams
- Rewritten as a **single, focused service page**: a hands-on, custom-curated
  learning framework the Enablement team runs *with* a team, built around that
  team's real work — with a clear **"be the champion — reach out"** action.

## AI Events Calendar
- Removed the redundant "Visit the AI Clinic" button.
- Particle artwork now forms a **calendar page** (with highlighted event days).

## Visual & theme polish (site-wide)
- **Light "dawn/dusk" theme toggle** with particle colors tuned for both modes.
- Improved **legibility** and fixed a tint that was dimming the whole site.
- **Self-hosted the gallery images** (no longer depending on an external repo).
- Several **bespoke particle "formations"** added to give each page its own
  identity (heart, calendar, play icon, staircase, medical sign) — all gently
  drifting/hovering rather than sitting static.

---

*Built on the project's 3D particle engine as a single-page site, so the living
background never reloads between pages — it morphs to match each one.*
